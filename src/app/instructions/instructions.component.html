Welcome!

Please read the following instructions carefully:

  • This study is part of a controlled experiment to better understand the efficacy of current design practices for instagram filters.

  • The expected completion time is 3-5 minutes. And your sincere contribution could lend you a gift voucher worth of $10 USD and help make the life of color blind people easier.

  • This study will consist of the following three sections.

Pre-Study User Form

In this section, you will be asked to provide your details like name, sex, age group, country, color blindness and email address. In addition to that, you need to provide information about your current experience with instagram filters.

Choosing Appropriate Filter Task

In this section, you will be shown two different images. For each one, you need to select a filter, that you think is most suitable for that image. There is no time constraint and you can try as many filters as you want.

Based on the collected data, filters that get selected most frequently will be considered as the most appropriate one for both the images. And three random participants who select the most appropriate filters for both images will receive the gift voucher.

Post-Study User Survey

In this section, you will be asked to share details of your task completion experience in a survey.

  • Please complete all three sections in a single continuous setting, rather than completing half once and then returning back to complete the rest.

  • After the submission of a section, it cannot be accessed again. Please DO NOT refresh or press back button. It will restart the application, and your incomplete submission data will get recorded.

  • You are kindly requested not to share the details of your response, either publicly or privately, with anyone at least till the completion of the study.
